Welcome to microletter!

Before you can start using your own installation of microletter, you need to complete the initial setup.

STEP 1 Email

To send your newsletter, you need to enter your Email credentials so your microletter installation can send Emails to your subscribers. Don't worry, microletter can only send Emails. It's recommended to create a seperate Email address to send your newsletter from.

You need to add these credentials in your Deta Space dashboard. Click on the three dots next to the microletter app icon, click on settings and enter the details there. Don't know where to find your SMTP credentials? Click here.
